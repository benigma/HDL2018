Hogarth Davies Lloyd is a Global Executive Search firm with offices in London, New York, Stockholm and Calgary. Established in 1995 by former banking and finance professionals, the firm has grown to become one of the pre-eminent providers of Executive Search services across Asset Management, Equities, Fixed Income, Currencies and Commodities, Investment Banking, Legal, Compliance & Regulatory, Private Banking and Wealth Management.

Hogarth Davies Lloyd is proud to work with a broad cross-section of clients including Major Global Investment Banks, Asset Managers, Hedge Funds, Sovereign Wealth Funds, Private Equity Firms, Commodity Trading Houses, Energy Companies and Major Non-Financial Corporations.

We operate a client focused approach across all projects that we undertake and we always seek to forge long-term partnerships with our clients. It is crucial for us to understand our clients' businesses, cultures, philosophies and strategies given our market facing role when engaging with and attracting the highest calibre candidates and businesses.

Hogarth Davies Lloyd has a proven and successful track record of working as a trusted adviser alongside our Financial Services clients and assisting them with everything from extensive strategic business build-outs to identifying specialists with unique skill sets to fill crucial niche roles.

We have invested significantly over the years in developing our research capabilities as well as our extensive network of industry contacts and we are frequently mandated by our clients to produce comprehensive business intelligence and market analysis documents in addition to our more traditional recruitment services.
